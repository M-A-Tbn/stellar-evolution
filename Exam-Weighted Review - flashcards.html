Question
What spectroscopic features distinguish supernova Types Ia, Ib, Ic, and II?
tap to flip
Answer
Type II shows hydrogen lines; Type I shows none. Among Type I, Ia shows Si II (thermonuclear WD explosion), Ib shows He but no Si (stripped-envelope core collapse), and Ic shows neither H, He, nor Si (fully stripped core collapse).
